# Projects

## 1. SuperStore Sales Dashboard 2022

### Project Overview
A business intelligence dashboard built to analyze SuperStore sales performance for 2022, focusing on revenue, profitability, regional performance, delayed orders, and category-level insights.

### Key KPIs
- Total Sales: **$733,284**
- Profit: **$93,431**
- Average Profit: **$28**
- Profit Margin: **12.74%**
- Total Orders: **1,687**
- Delayed Orders: **904+**

### Dashboard Insights
- Strong year-over-year quarterly sales growth
- Q4 was the highest-performing quarter
- Technology was the most profitable category
- Furniture showed weak profitability despite strong sales
- Texas, Illinois, and Pennsylvania were major loss-making states
- Philadelphia was a significant loss-making city
- More than 27% of orders were delayed

### Files Included
- `SuperStore Sales 2022.twbx`
- `SuperStore Sales 2022 Insights.pdf`
- Dashboard Screenshot

## 2. Games Sales Dashboard

### Project Overview
A Tableau dashboard analyzing game sales performance, profitability, shipping performance, customer behavior, and regional trends.

### Key KPIs
- Total Sales: **₹22,97,354**
- Net Revenue: **₹19,74,763**
- Profit: **₹2,86,347**
- Profit Margin: **14.50%**
- Total Orders: **5,009**
- Quantity Sold: **37,873**
- COGS: **₹16,88,416**

### Dashboard Insights
- Sales peaked in Q4 2019
- Business experienced a sharp 58% decline after the peak
- West and East regions dominate revenue contribution
- PS5 and XBox showed weak profitability
- Shipping delays remain a major operational issue
- High-value customers identified for retention strategy
- Multiple cities consistently generated losses

### Files Included
- `Games Sales.twbx`
- `Games Sales Insights.pdf`
- Dashboard Screenshot
